Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Hub?

AI Hub is a curated directory of the best AI tools and resources available online. We help you discover and compare artificial intelligence solutions for various needs, from text generation and image creation to code assistance and productivity tools.

How do you categorize tools?

We categorize tools based on their primary function. Our categories include:

  • Text & Writing - Content generation, writing assistants, copywriting tools
  • Image & Art - AI image generators, art creation, photo editing
  • Video & Animation - Video generation, animation, editing tools
  • Audio & Music - Voice generation, music creation, audio editing
  • Code & Development - Code assistants, IDE plugins, development tools
  • Productivity - Task management, automation, workflow tools
  • Marketing & SEO - SEO tools, marketing automation, analytics
  • Analytics & Data - Data visualization, business intelligence tools
  • Automation & Agents - AI agents, workflow automation
  • Research & Learning - Research tools, learning platforms
  • Design & UI/UX - UI design, prototyping, UX research
  • Customer Support - Chatbots, support tools, helpdesk
  • E-commerce - Product recommendations, sales tools

What do the pricing types mean?

We use four pricing categories:

  • Free - Completely free to use without any limitations or payments required.
  • Freemium - Free tier available with optional paid upgrades for advanced features.
  • Paid - Requires payment to use. May offer trials or subscriptions.
  • Open Source - Free to use, modify, and deploy yourself. Source code is publicly available.

Is AI Hub free to use?

Yes! AI Hub is completely free to use. We help you discover AI tools at no cost. We may earn a small commission when you sign up for certain tools through our links, but this doesn't affect our recommendations or increase your cost.

How often do you update the tool database?

We regularly update our database with new AI tools and remove tools that are no longer available or have shut down. Our team continuously researches and evaluates new AI solutions to keep our directory current and relevant.

Can I submit a tool to be listed?

We're always looking to expand our directory with quality AI tools. If you have a tool you'd like to recommend, please reach out to us through our contact channels.

How do you choose which tools to feature?

We evaluate tools based on several factors including functionality, ease of use, pricing transparency, user reviews, and overall quality. We aim to feature a diverse range of tools across different categories and price points.

Do you review or rate the tools?

We provide ratings based on user feedback and our own evaluation criteria. Our ratings are meant to help you make informed decisions, but we encourage you to try tools firsthand to determine which best fits your needs.
